Our story

We believe good food and kindness to the planet can be served together.

A modern kitchen with a community spirit.

Green Fork started with the simple idea that vegan food should be vibrant, welcoming and delicious.

Every recipe reflects a balance of vegetables, whole grains and plant proteins, crafted to please regulars and first-time guests alike.

Fresh, local ingredients

We build our menu around the freshest available produce, with minimal processing and maximum flavour.

Inclusive hospitality

Our restaurant is accessible, comfortable and welcoming for guests with allergies or dietary preferences.

Sustainable operations

We reduce waste, reuse where possible, and support suppliers who share our ecological values.
